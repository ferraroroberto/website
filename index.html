Virtual meetings can be exhausting. You know the feeling: wondering if people are paying attention, struggling to sound confident through the screen, or just wishing you could connect better in those tiny Zoom boxes 😩


I've been there too.


Since 2020, I've spent over 5,000 hours in virtual meetings. Along the way, I've learned that being effective online is about so much more than just having a great microphone or camera, and I LOVE a good tech setup 🤖


In this workshop, I'll share how to make virtual meetings effective, engaging, and fun!

What will you get?

Memorable First Impressions

Learn how to make a stronger, more memorable impression from the moment you join a call.

Engaging Communication

Discover how to use your voice, gestures, and presence to keep people engaged.

Avoid Common Pitfalls

Avoid common mistakes that can derail even the best-prepared sessions.

Technical Mastery

And yes, we'll talk about the all-important technical setup, too!
